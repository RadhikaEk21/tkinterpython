Tkinter
* Tkinter is the most commonly used library for developing 
  GUI (Graphical User Interface) in Python.
* Tk and Tkinter are available on most of the Unix platforms as well as on the Windows system, 
  developing GUI applications with Tkinter becomes the fastest and easiest.

syntax:-

root = Tk()  
#what content  to be displayed

root.mainloop()
